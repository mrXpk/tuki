Our Story

A Cup of Tea,
A Community of People

At TUKI, we believe great moments begin with a great cup of tea. Inspired by local culture and built around community, we provide a cozy space where friends gather, ideas flow, and every sip brings comfort.

Whether you're stopping by for a quiet breakfast, a catch-up with old friends, or simply a moment of calm — TUKI welcomes you with warmth and authentic hospitality.
